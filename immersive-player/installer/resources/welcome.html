Welcome to Immersive Player

This installer will guide you through the installation of Immersive Player, a GPU-accelerated HAP video player with advanced projection output features.

Key Features:

  • HAP, HAP Alpha, and HAP Q video playback
  • Multi-output support for multiple screens/projectors
  • Edge blending for seamless multi-projector setups
  • Geometric warping for projection surface alignment
  • Built-in HAP video converter
  • Aqueduct network streaming
